About The Event

TechnoNova is strictly not just another tech fest; it's a celebration of engineering excellence and creative prowess. Organized by the diligent student council of SVIT, it brings together the brightest minds from across the state to compete, learn, and grow.

Whether you are a coder, a gamer, a roboticist, or an artist, TechnoNova has something for everyone. From high-stakes hackathons to mesmerizing cultural nights, prepare for an unforgettable experience.
